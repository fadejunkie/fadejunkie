## Body

Most buyers don't know this is even an option. When you work with a buyer's agent, that agent earns a commission paid by the seller. At Weichert Corwin, we share part of that with our buyers — a cash rebate applied directly at closing.

It's our way of saying thank you for trusting us with something this important.

### How it works

1. **Connect with us** — reach out and let us know you're looking to buy in the New Braunfels area
2. **We represent you** — we handle your search, negotiations, inspections, and closing from start to finish
3. **You close, you earn** — at closing, your rebate is applied as a credit toward your costs

That's it. No hoops. No fine print that cancels it out.

The buyer rebate is available on most residential purchases in our market area — New Braunfels, Canyon Lake, Gruene, Spring Branch, Seguin, and surrounding Comal County communities. A few transaction types (certain new builds, for example) may vary — we'll be upfront about that from day one.

## FAQ

**What is a buyer rebate?**
A buyer rebate is cash returned to the buyer at closing from the agent's commission. It reduces your out-of-pocket closing costs.

**Does the rebate affect my loan or financing?**
It depends on your lender and loan type. Most conventional and cash buyers can apply it without issue. VA and FHA loans have specific rules — we'll walk you through what applies to your situation before you make any commitments.

**Does using a rebate mean I get less service?**
No. You get full representation — property search, offer strategy, negotiation, inspection coordination, and closing support. The rebate doesn't change what we do for you.

**Is the rebate available on new construction?**
Some builder transactions pay reduced commissions, which can affect the rebate amount. We'll always tell you exactly what to expect before you go under contract.
