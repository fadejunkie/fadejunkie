## H2: Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the rebate reduce the level of service I receive?
Not at all. You get full-service buyer representation — showings, negotiations, inspections, contract management, closing coordination. The rebate comes from our commission, not from cutting corners.

### Can I use the rebate toward closing costs?
Yes. Many of our buyers apply the rebate directly to closing costs, which can significantly reduce or eliminate out-of-pocket expenses at the closing table.

### Does the rebate apply to new construction?
Yes — and this is where we stand apart. Most agents don't offer rebates on new construction because builders already set the commission. We pass savings to you regardless.

### Do I need to do anything special to qualify?
Just choose Weichert Corwin as your buyer's agent before you start touring homes. If you're visiting a builder's model home, let us schedule that first visit with you so we can register as your representation.

### How is the rebate paid out?
The rebate appears as a credit on your closing disclosure (CD) at the closing table. Your lender will see it as a standard closing credit. No separate check, no waiting period.
