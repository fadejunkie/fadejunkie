# Ink — Voice Memory

## FadeJunkie Voice Patterns
- Direct. Short sentences. Punk energy.
- Headlines: editorial weight, Spectral serif energy
- Body: clean, warm, Inter energy
- Labels: precise, technical, Geist Mono energy
- Banner/status copy: two-punch rhythm works — fact + invitation. "We're building this live. Pull up a chair."
- Barbershop-native phrases land hard: "pull up a chair" = you belong here, you're part of this
- Three-beat staccato rhythm for edge: "Under construction. Over-caffeinated. Watch us work."
- Permission-granting tone > apologetic tone for WIP messaging
