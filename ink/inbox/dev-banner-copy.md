# Revise Copy: Development Banner

## Context
We're adding a slim maroon sticky banner to the top of fadejunkie.com while the site is under active development. It needs one punchy line that tells visitors: **the site is being built in public and they should stick around to watch it evolve.**

## Draft Copy
> This site is being developed — tune in and play around to watch us grow in real time.

## What We Need
Tighten this up in fadejunkie's voice — **direct, punk, culture-native.** Not corporate, not apologetic. This isn't a "pardon our dust" message. It's an invitation: we're building something and you get a front-row seat.

## Constraints
- Single line — it lives in a slim top-of-page banner
- Short enough to not wrap on mobile
- Should feel confident, not under-construction-clipart energy
