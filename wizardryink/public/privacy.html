Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Wizardry Ink Tattoo Studio ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us through our booking inquiry form and communications:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number
  • Social media — Instagram handle (optional)
  • Tattoo details — placement, size, style, design description, reference photos
  • Health information — relevant health conditions you voluntarily disclose for safety purposes
  • Date of birth — to verify you are 18 years of age or older

We may also automatically collect basic usage data when you visit our website (such as browser type and pages visited) through standard web analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to booking inquiries and schedule appointments
  • To communicate with you about your appointment, design, and aftercare
  • To maintain internal records of studio clients and bookings
  • To verify age eligibility (18+ required for all services)
  • To improve our website and studio services

Reference Photos

Photos you submit as reference images are used solely to understand your tattoo vision. They are stored securely and are not shared publicly or with third parties without your consent.

Photo Release

We may photograph completed tattoos for portfolio and promotional use on our social media and website. You may opt out of this at any time by notifying us before or after your appointment.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • With service providers who help us operate our booking and communication systems (bound by confidentiality obligations)
  • When required by law or to protect the safety of our clients and staff

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and maintain accurate studio records. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to function properly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Children's Privacy

Our services are strictly for individuals 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
